Hi! I'm a sophomore Aerospace Engineering student at Texas A&M, originally from the Bay Area. I enjoy learning more about the world around me - a key driver behind my love for engineering - and the reason for why I have picked up an interest in psychology and philosophy. Within aerospace, I am looking to pursue a career in eVTOL (see VFD) with a clean mix of work in design & modeling and building & troubleshooting.
